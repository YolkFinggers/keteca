Green Laser Marking System

The Keteca M series green laser marking system is featured with compact structures design ensuring great performance
and beam transmission, engineered for demanding 24/7 industrial applications. It is designed for requiring short pulse
widths, excellent beam quality and high intensity pulses over a wide range of operating conditions.
‍
“Green laser”, is a high-power Q-switched laser system. Working with a galvo scanner, high speed cornering with minimal
“burn-in” is achieved. And with excellent power and performance at different pulse repetition frequency, a variety
applications can be achieved with high speeds and small focus spot sizes. The shorter pulse widths coupled with low
energy / high rep rate laser output provides very clean and effective marking, scribing or ablations.

Key Features

Superior pulse-to-pulse stability

Low cost of ownership

High peak power and short pulse width

Field proven long life pump diode

Applications

LED manufacturing

Ceramic Scribing

Wafer Manufacturing

Plastics marking

Ultra-thin metal film processing

PCB & flex PCB Cutting & Marking

Specifications:

Laser power, 7/8/14 Watts

Wavelength 532nm

Pulse repetition rate, 1-150kHz

Pulse width 15 nsec

Marking area 70x70 / 110x110mm

Input power, 220-240VAC, 50-60Hz, 1Phase
